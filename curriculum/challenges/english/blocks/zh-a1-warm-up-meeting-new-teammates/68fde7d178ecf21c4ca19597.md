### --feedback--

This means "may I ask", and is often used to politely ask a question.

---

### --feedback--

# --explanation--

The phrase `我是 (wǒ shì)` is used to introduce one's name. For example, `我是王华 (wǒ shì Wáng Huá)` means "I am Wang Hua".
